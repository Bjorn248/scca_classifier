Welcome to the SCCA Solo Classification Assistant!

Please choose the make, model, and year of your car. The classes you are eligible for will be highlighted.

If your car not listed then you should attempt to class your car using the NOC (Not Otherwise Classified) rules.
Please refer to the SCCA rulebook if this happens, or get in contact with an experienced autocrosser to help you out!

Choose your class below. Classes are roughly in order from least to most modified, from left to right.
